Awesome Broccoli-Cheese Casserole

Description

My mom made this broccoli cheese casserole every Thanksgiving when I was little. We kids could never get enough! If you have children or have some coming to visit you as guests this Thanksgiving, I guarantee that they will eat (and enjoy) this veggie dish. It's also fabulous with a Christmas ham.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 egg,beaten
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onnion
  • 3 (10 ounce) packages of frozen, chopped, brocolli
  • 8 ounces of shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • salt and pepperto taste
  • 1 dash of paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly butter a 9X13-inch casserole dish
  2. Whiskcondensed soup, mayonnaise, egg, and onions together in a medium mixing bowl until combined.
  3. Place frozen brocoli into a very large mixing bowl and break it up if necessary; add soup mixture and mix well to coat. Sprinkle with cheese and mix well; spread mixture into the prepared baking dish. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  4. Bake in thepreheated ovenfor 45 minutes to 1 hour.
